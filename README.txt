shopclips - Adds utilities that helps shopclips work better with WooCommerce stores.

== Description ==

> **Note:** This plugin is only really of use to you if you have a shopclips account.

> shopclips is a paid service and it requires access to WooCommerce store data (products, orders and customers) to be functional. You can sign up for shopclips ([here](https://verbo.ai/pricing)). Once you've selected a plan, we'll reach out to you to connect your store to our platform and setup the shopclips experience for iOS and Android. For more information please refer to our ([FAQ](https://verbo.ai/pricing#faq)) section and our ([privacy policy](https://verbo.ai/privacy-policy)).

shopclips lets your customers skip the hassle of managing their subscriptions. Here's how:

= Make physical objects shoppable =
Add QR codes, NFC tags to your packaging, stickers or magnets, so your customers can scan or tap to re-order whenever they're running low. We provide you with QR codes that you can easily stick or print on your packets.

= Make replenishment as easy as a single tap =
shopclips enables your customers to replenish frequently purchased goods with 1-tap. No app or website needed.

= Send Renewal SMS Reminders: (coming soon) =
Our replenishment AI predicts when your customers need to reorder, and sends a 1-tap repurchase link via SMS or Whatsapp.

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.
